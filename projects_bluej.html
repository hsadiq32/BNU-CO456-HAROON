creating an adventure.

just a blueJ project

For this project, I was tasked with creating a new small version of Zuul to use as proof of concept. The game allows individuals to indulge in the crazy world of monsters, where one needs to either kill or be killed to survive. The player must travel through different areas of the game battling through foes and enemies to reach the end, while scavenging for hidden treasures spread amongst the grounds of the game. Beware once in the land of this Zuul game the only way out is to win.This game is based on the classic console based game Colossal Cave Adventure by Will Crowther (1975)
